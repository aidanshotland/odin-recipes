Ramen

Description

This Ramen is extremely easy to make along with extremely yummy

  • 1 package top ramen
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • Brown sugar
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put ramen in water
  3. Heat butter on low heat
  4. Add soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar
  5. Add ramen to sauce
  6. Sprinkle red pepper flakes
